# Can't deactivate license key

If you have encountered the `Failed to deactivate license` error message when attempting to deactivate your license key, it is worth trying a simple solution first. Hold down the `ALT` (Windows) or `Option` (Mac) key when you click the `Deactivate` button. This will override the deactivation process and forcibly remove the license key from your site.

If the problem persists, please [contact our support team](https://www.gravitykit.com/support) for further assistance.
