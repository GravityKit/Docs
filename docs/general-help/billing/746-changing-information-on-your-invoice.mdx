# Changing information on your invoice

You can change the information on your invoice by following the steps below.

#### **1) Go to your [GravityKit Account page](https://www.gravitykit.com/account/)**

...and click on the Billing &amp; Invoices tab. On the Billing History section, find the payment you want to modify its information and click on the "View Invoice" link.

#### ![Screenshot of the account page showing the Billings & Invoices tab](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/54c58489e4b051242988532c/images/63f550bcc490cd5d5b96a0d6/file-YVORXXoW7y.png)2) On the invoice details page, click the "Update" button under the INVOICE TO section:

#### ![Screenshot showing the invoice editing screen](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/54c58489e4b051242988532c/images/63f55105c490cd5d5b96a0d7/file-Tc8kSsrZFo.png)3) Add or modify your company, billing address, and tax information:

![Screenshot showing the information fields](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/54c58489e4b051242988532c/images/63f55191b7da6b15a7ea191d/file-o2V4pyUbE3.png)

After filling in the fields, make sure to click on the "Save billing details &amp; generate invoice" button down the page. You'll be redirected back to the invoice details page.

#### 4) Print or download a PDF of the updated invoice

![Screenshot showing the invoice with new information](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/54c58489e4b051242988532c/images/63f551cd3c396c395ec0ec82/file-TQUScpAcaW.png)

If you need further assistance with an invoice, make sure to contact us: [https://www.gravitykit.com/support/](https://www.gravitykit.com/support/).
