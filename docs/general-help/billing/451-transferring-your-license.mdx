# Transferring your license

Want to transfer your license to another person or organization? No problem! Simply get in touch with us here on the [GravityKit Support page.](https://www.gravitykit.com/support/)

When contacting us, be sure to include the following information:

- The **current email** to which the license is assigned
- The **new email** and **name** of the person you would like to transfer your license to
- Your **GravityKit License Key,** which you can find on the [GravityKit Account page](https://www.gravitykit.com/account/)

**Note:** Licenses cannot be resold, only transferred!
