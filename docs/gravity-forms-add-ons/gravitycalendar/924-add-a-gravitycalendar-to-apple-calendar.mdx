# Add a GravityCalendar to Apple Calendar

In this article, we'll show you how to sync an existing GravityCalendar with your Apple Calendar. This means that all events on your GravityCalendar will also show up on your Apple Calendar.

The first step is to enable the "calendar subscription link" feature in your existing GravityCalendar feed.

![The GravityCalendar subscription link with a button that says 'Copy to clipboard'](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/54c58489e4b051242988532c/images/64099a2b023a35108b661e28/file-xzYLYFUQer.png)

Now, head over to your Apple Calendar, click "File" and then "New Calendar Subscription". Paste your GravityCalendar subscription link into the box and click "Subscribe".

![A input box for an external calendar URL in Apple Calendar](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/54c58489e4b051242988532c/images/6409a5b374c58f66ad0cfe5b/file-jeMfOqzkJi.png)Now, you can configure the calendar settings, including how often you want the calendar to auto-refresh. When you're done, click "OK".

![The calendar subscription settings in Apple Calendar](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/54c58489e4b051242988532c/images/6409a5f9023a35108b661e35/file-AYdvCfwqCo.png)That's it! Your GravityCalendar will now sync with your Apple Calendar.
