# Modifying Date, Time, or Entry Date field formats



### Here's that pesky date you want to change:

![](https://www.gravitykit.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/01/here-s-that-pesky-date-you-want-to-change-.png?1424818680)

### Click the Settings icon next to the Date or Time field you would like to modify

![](https://www.gravitykit.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/01/click-the-settings-icon-next-to-the-date-or-time-field-you-would-like-to-modify.png?1424818685)

### Enter the date format you want.

For more information on the date format, [read this article about formatting the date &amp; time](https://codex.wordpress.org/Formatting_Date_and_Time).

![](https://www.gravitykit.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/01/enter-the-date-format-you-want.png?1424818691)

### Click the "Close" button

![](https://www.gravitykit.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/01/click-the-close-button.png?1424818695)

### Save the View

![](https://www.gravitykit.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/01/save-the-view.png?1424818701)

### Your date will now be formatted as you defined!

And there you go, the date, as you like it:

![](https://www.gravitykit.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/01/your-date-will-now-be-formatted-as-you-defined-.png?1424818706)
