# Adding a percent sign (%) to a column

To add a percent sign to a data column, first add a Custom Content field to your view:

![Adding a Custom Content field to a View](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/54c58489e4b051242988532c/images/5d950ade04286364bc8fe149/file-0flSOvdO45.png)

For the field you wish to display the percent sign next to, insert that field's Merge Tag into the Custom Content box followed by the percent sign like so:

![Adding a percent sign next to the merge tag for a number field](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/54c58489e4b051242988532c/images/5d950b5f2c7d3a7e9ae1f7a6/file-P8qV7TiMfL.png)
