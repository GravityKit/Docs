# Getting Started With the Social Sharing & SEO Extension

In this article, we’ll show you how to get set up with Social Sharing and SEO extension.

## Installing the Social Sharing &amp; SEO Extension

You can install the Social Sharing &amp; SEO extension from inside WordPress. Simply hover over "GravityKit", click "Products &amp; Licenses", scroll down to "Social Sharing &amp; SEO" and click "Install Now".

![The Install Now button for the GravityView Social Sharing & SEO extension](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/54c58489e4b051242988532c/images/63a569fe19e761267717239e/file-PywYFRH7Rn.png)

You can now configure social share buttons and integrate your Views with Yoast for better SEO.

## Configuring Social Share Buttons

Social Sharing and SEO allows you to add Jetpack social sharing buttons to the entries in your View. To configure social sharing buttons, start by installing the free Jetpack plugin for WordPress.

![The Jetpack plugin preview in WordPress](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/54c58489e4b051242988532c/images/62333850c1e53608cf9e9ab5/file-PMMaQJm4Ka.png)

After installing and activating the plugin, follow [this helpful guide](https://docs.gravitykit.com/article/352-setting-up-social-sharing-seo) to configure social sharing buttons.

## Improving Your SEO

Social Sharing &amp; SEO also allows you to integrate GravityView with Yoast SEO, the most popular WordPress SEO plugin. To configure the integration, start by installing the Yoast SEO plugin.

![The Yoast SEO plugin preview in WordPress](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/54c58489e4b051242988532c/images/623338652ce7ed0fb091698e/file-WWWD0hzJgO.png)

When you’ve installed and activated Yoast, follow [this helpful guide](https://docs.gravitykit.com/article/725-setting-up-seo-for-views-with-yoast-seo) to finish configuring the integration and start improving your SEO.
