# How to enable and configure map marker clustering

## Maps 1.6 added the ability to display multiple maps as a "cluster"; a single icon showing the number of markers in the area.

In this article, you will learn how to enable and configure marker clustering for your View.

## Where there are multiple markers, they will be clustered based on your settings.

![Where there are multiple markers, they will be clustered based on your settings.](https://www.gravitykit.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/10/where-there-are-multiple-markers-they-will-be-clustered-based-on-your-settings.png)

## When editing your View, click on the Maps tab of the View Settings box

![When editing your View, click on the Maps tab of the View Settings box](https://www.gravitykit.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/10/when-editing-your-view-click-on-the-maps-tab-of-the-view-settings-box.png)

## Scroll to the bottom of the Map settings and check the Marker Clustering checkbox

![Scroll to the bottom of the Map settings and check the Marker Clustering checkbox](https://www.gravitykit.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/10/scroll-to-the-bottom-of-the-map-settings-and-check-the-marker-clustering-checkbox.png)

## Scroll down some more and set the Clustering Max Zoom

![Scroll down some more and set the Clustering Max Zoom](https://www.gravitykit.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/10/scroll-down-some-more-and-set-the-clustering-max-zoom.png)

To better understand what impacts this setting has, [check out this example page](https://googlemaps.github.io/js-marker-clusterer/examples/advanced_example.html) and change the "Max zoom level" setting, then click the "Refresh Map" button.

## The Max Zoom Level setting prevents showing clusters when zoomed in past a certain level

![The Max Zoom Level setting prevents showing clusters when zoomed in past a certain level](https://www.gravitykit.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/10/the-max-zoom-level-setting-prevents-showing-clusters-when-zoomed-in-past-a-certain-level.png)

If you want to not show clusters when zoomed in close on a city, set a higher number. If you want to show clusters even when zoomed all the way in, set a higher number.

**This map is zoomed in at level 6,** with the Max Zoom set to level 16.

## Max Zoom Level 5

![Max Zoom Level 5](https://www.gravitykit.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/10/max-zoom-level-5.png)

When we change the Max Zoom to level 5, because we are zoomed in at level 6, the clusters no longer appear.
