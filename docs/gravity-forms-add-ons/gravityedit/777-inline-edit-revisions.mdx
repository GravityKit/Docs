# Settings for GravityEdit


:::caution

GravityEdit support was added in GravityRevisions version 1.1.

:::



The [GravityRevisions add-on](https://www.gravitykit.com/extensions/entry-revisions/) tracks changes made by the [GravityEdit add-on](https://www.gravitykit.com/extensions/gravityview-inline-edit/) by default. This default setting can be changed by updating a setting.

### Modifying the “Default Inline Edit Behavior” setting

1. Log in to the WordPress Dashboard
2. Under the Forms menu, click on the Settings link
3. Click on Entry Revisions
4. Update the setting under the "Default Inline Edit Behavior"
5. Click Save Settings

![Screenshot of the Entry Revisions settings screen in Gravity Forms](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/54c58489e4b051242988532c/images/611c29c7b37d837a3d0e4b2e/file-jyPv40yhT2.jpg)


:::info

If you don't see the setting, the [GravityEdit add-on](https://www.gravitykit.com/extensions/gravityview-inline-edit/) may not be active.

:::

 ### Modifying the “Inline Edit Behavior” setting per-form

The default setting applies to all forms unless you override the setting for an individual form.

1. Log into the WordPress Dashboard
2. Under the Forms menu, click on Forms
3. Click on the form you would like to update settings for
4. Hover over the "Settings" link in the form toolbar and click on the Form Settings  
    ![Hovering over the form's Settings menu](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/54c58489e4b051242988532c/images/611c2bdb21ef206e5592c6e9/file-Y7QyUr6zbE.jpg)
5. Scroll down to the Form Options section
6. Update the setting under the "Inline Edit Behavior"
7. Click Save Settings


:::info

If you don't see the setting, the [GravityEdit add-on](https://www.gravitykit.com/extensions/gravityview-inline-edit/) may not be active.

:::
